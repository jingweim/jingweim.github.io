👩🏻‍💻 About me

I'm a 2nd-year Ph.D. student at the UW Graphics and Imaging Laboratory (GRAIL) working with Prof. Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman, Prof. Steve Seitz and Prof. Brian Curless. Previously, I studied computer science at MIT and had the honor to work with Prof. Antonio Torralba and Prof. Phillip Isola.

My current research interests are 3D Reconstruction and Neural Rendering. My ongoing and past projects involve Neural Rendering, Image Forensics, Image Synthesis, and 3D Reconstruction.
